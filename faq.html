Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a route?

Fill in the start and end address, add one or more destinations, then click "Calculate Route" or "Log Route" to save it.

Can I use this app offline?

Yes! You can create and view saved routes offline. Calculating new routes requires an internet connection.

How is Net Profit calculated?

Net Profit = Earnings – Fuel Cost – Maintenance – Supplies. Drive Time and Total Hours are also factored in for profitability per hour.

Where is my data stored?

Your trip logs are stored securely in your account using Cloudflare KV, and backed up in your browser’s local storage.

How can I reset my password?

Click “Reset Password” on the sign-in screen. You’ll need the reset key shown when you first signed up.

Who do I contact for help?

You can reach out at support@gorouteyourself.com.
