Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2, 2025

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers

Note: While we do not collect personal identifiers, route logs saved by users may contain address and earnings information they choose to enter. This information is stored only for their own use.

2. Offline and Online Storage

The App stores some data locally on your device—such as route drafts and user preferences—for a smoother offline experience. If you sign in, your saved route logs are securely transmitted and stored on our servers using encrypted connections. This data is used solely to provide your personalized experience and is never shared with third parties.

3. Internet Access

The App requires internet access to load maps, calculate routes, and sync saved data (if you're logged in). This communication occurs securely over HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services including:

  • Google Maps API: for map rendering and directions. Address information entered for routing may be sent to Google.
  • Browser storage (localStorage): for saving logs and offline data

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously. All communication between the App and our servers occurs over secure HTTPS connections. Route logs are stored in your browser's localStorage and—if you're signed in—securely saved to our servers to ensure access across sessions and devices. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

This App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Account Information

When you create an account, we store a securely hashed version of your password and a unique token to authenticate your access. We do not store plaintext passwords. Your account data is used only for managing your saved logs and enabling access across devices.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated logs at any time from within the App. Once deleted, this data is permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted here on this page.
